Ayurveda in Brief

Ayurveda, the “science of life,” describes health as a dynamic balance of three organizing principles—Vata, Pitta, and Kapha—called doshas. Each person has a unique combination at baseline (their Prakriti). Day-to-day life can shift how the doshas express (your Vikriti).

Doshas

  • Vata (air/space): light, dry, cool, mobile. When imbalanced, may show dryness, restlessness, variable appetite.
  • Pitta (fire/water): hot, sharp, intense. Imbalance can show as irritability, overheating, strong appetite, loose stools.
  • Kapha (earth/water): heavy, slow, stable, cool. Imbalance may present as sluggishness, congestion, weight gain.

Prakriti vs. Vikriti

Prakriti is your “set point.” Vikriti is your current state. If they differ, you support the dosha that’s currently elevated (Vikriti) to come back toward your Prakriti.
