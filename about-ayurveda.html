Ayurveda Overview

Ayurveda in Brief

The science of life: balancing Vata, Pitta, and Kapha for well-being.

Ayurveda, the “science of life,” describes health as a dynamic balance of three organizing principles—Vata, Pitta, and Kapha—called doshas. Each person has a unique combination at baseline (their Prakriti). Day-to-day life can shift how the doshas express (your Vikriti).

The Three Doshas

Vata (air/space)

Light, dry, cool, mobile. Imbalance may show dryness, restlessness, variable appetite.

Pitta (fire/water)

Hot, sharp, intense. Imbalance can appear as irritability, overheating, strong appetite, loose stools.

Kapha (earth/water)

Heavy, slow, stable, cool. Imbalance may present as sluggishness, congestion, weight gain.

Prakriti vs. Vikriti

Prakriti is your “set point.” Vikriti is your current state. If they differ, support the dosha that’s currently elevated (Vikriti) to move back toward your Prakriti.
