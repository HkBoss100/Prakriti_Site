Frequently Asked Questions

Can my Prakriti change?
Prakriti is considered constant; however, your current state (Vikriti) can shift with season, lifestyle, and stress.
What if my Prakriti and Vikriti are different?
Support the currently elevated dosha (Vikriti) with opposite qualities until balance returns.
Is this a medical diagnosis?
No. This is an educational tool only. For medical issues, see a qualified professional.
Can I use these tips with my current diet?
Yes—start small. Favor foods and routines with opposite qualities to the dosha that’s elevated.
What if I get a tie (dual dosha)?
You can apply a mix of suggestions. Notice which set feels most balancing and lean that way.
