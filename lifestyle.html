Vata — Ground & Warm

Light, dry, cool, mobile. Aim for warmth, routine, nourishment.

Routine

  • Regular meals and bedtime; avoid skipping meals.
  • Warm oil self-massage (abhyanga) before a shower.
  • Keep a gentle, predictable daily rhythm.

Food

  • Favor warm, moist foods (khichdi, stews, soups).
  • Healthy fats (ghee, sesame oil, avocado).
  • Spices like ginger, cumin, ajwain in moderation.

Movement

  • Slow yoga, walking, light strength work.
  • Avoid overexertion; keep warm in wind/cold.

Mind

  • Breathwork with long exhales; short meditations.
  • Calming music, journaling, and pauses between tasks.

Pitta — Cool & Soothe

Hot, sharp, oily. Aim for cooling, moderation, and pauses.

Routine

  • Don’t skip meals; eat on time to avoid irritability.
  • Take cooling breaks; avoid midday peak sun when possible.
  • Build in non-competitive downtime daily.

Food

  • Favor sweet/bitter/astringent tastes (cucumber, gourds, leafy greens).
  • Cooling drinks (room-temp water, coconut, rose water sparingly).
  • Go easy on chilies, fried foods, vinegar, alcohol.

Movement

  • Moderate intensity: swimming, evening walks, yoga.
  • Keep competition low; avoid overheating.

Mind

  • Cooling breath (sheetali/sheetkari), guided relaxations.
  • Gratitude journaling; time in nature/greens.

Kapha — Lighten & Invigorate

Heavy, cool, stable. Aim for stimulation, warmth, variety.

Routine

  • Wake early; reduce long daytime naps.
  • Keep rooms bright; open windows; seek morning light.
  • Choose active breaks over passive scrolling.

Food

  • Favor light, warm, spicy foods; lots of veggies and legumes.
  • Limit heavy, cold, and very sweet foods; watch portions.
  • Ginger/lemon water; spices like black pepper and mustard seed.

Movement

  • Daily moderate-to-brisk activity; intervals, stairs, cycling.
  • Warm-up thoroughly; sweat a little most days.

Mind

  • Upbeat music, social workouts, energizing breath (bhastrika gently).
  • Declutter spaces to avoid inertia.
